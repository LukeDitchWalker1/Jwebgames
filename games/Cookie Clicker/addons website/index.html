Directions

Find the addon you want, then click on the "Copy" button next to it
Then, go to the bookmarks bar and right click, now press "Add Page..."
Then, on the URL bar, delete everything in it, then press CTRL + V
Name the bookmark what you want, I prefer it being the addon name
Save it, Open Cookie Clicker, then open the bookmark! Enjoy!
